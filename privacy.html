Privacy

LAST UPDATED · JULY 2026

The short version

This website doesn't collect your personal information. No accounts, no forms, no tracking pixels, no selling data. It's a static page that tells our story.

What we don't collect

  • We don't ask for your name, email, or payment details — ever, anywhere on this site.
  • We don't run analytics trackers or advertising pixels.
  • We don't use cookies.

Donations happen elsewhere — on purpose

When you click a give button, you leave this website and go to the official fundraising platform of SOS Children's Villages USA. Any information you provide there (name, email, payment details) is collected by SOS Children's Villages and their payment processor — not by us. We never see or touch your payment information or your money. Their privacy policy governs that transaction: sos-usa.org/privacy-policy.

Hosting

This site is hosted on Netlify, which may log standard technical request data (like IP addresses) to serve and secure the site, as described in Netlify's privacy policy. Fonts are served by Google Fonts.

Questions

Reach out through any of the social channels linked from our page.
