№ 01 OUR MISSION

One million dollars — to those who need it most.

What we're doing

The Parrish Foundation exists to raise money for children and families who grew up less fortunate — the orphaned, the abandoned, and the ones who never got a fair hand at life. Our first goal is to generate $1,000,000 for them, and every dollar of it is tracked in public, right here.

Why we're doing it

Some people are dealt incredible cards — a good family, a safe home, people who believe in them. That kind of fortune isn't earned; it's given. And we believe what's given is meant to be given back.

This foundation is that belief, made real. Not someday, not once we're big — now, out in the open, one dollar at a time. The giving is the whole point, and the receipts are the proof.

More questions, answered

Who is Mr. Parrish?

J. R. Parrish is the mentor this foundation is named after — the person who taught our founder, Sam Duffy, what the cards he'd been dealt were really for. His belief in giving back is the seed everything here grew from.

How did we meet Mr. Parrish?

The full story of that first meeting — and how it set this whole mission in motion — is one we'll be sharing here soon.

Who's on the board of the foundation?

The Executive Board of The Parrish Foundation was founded by Sam Duffy, and includes J. R. Parrish, Owen G. Smyth, Collin N. Davis, Nathan D. Goodman, and Sebastian C. Arnold. You'll find them listed at the bottom of every page.

№ 02 CHARITABLE GOALS

The first goal: one million dollars.

Where we are right now

The Parrish Foundation isn't a fully established nonprofit yet. Building one the right way — the legal structure, the accountability, the team — takes real investment, and we won't cut corners or rush it. So for now, every dollar we raise goes directly to SOS Children's Villages USA. It never passes through our hands, and all of it is tracked in public, right here.

Why, and when we'll launch

The launch is held up by one thing: investment. Standing up a real foundation costs money we're still raising — and we'd rather do it right than do it fast. The day we have what it takes to run it properly, the Foundation goes live on its own. Until then, the giving never waits; it just runs through the best partner in the world at this work.

The goal

$1,000,000 generated for children who need a loving home — the orphaned and abandoned kids who were dealt the worst cards. That's the number on the front page, and every dollar toward it is counted in public.

It's the first of many. As the mission grows, the goals grow with it — more raised, more good done, and one day the Parrish Foundation standing on its own. But it all starts here, with the first million.

The Parrish Foundation, fully established

When the Foundation stands on its own, the mission doesn't just continue — it multiplies. This is the number we are ultimately building toward:

$1,000,000,000

One billion dollars raised for children and families who grew up less fortunate. The first million is only the starting line.
