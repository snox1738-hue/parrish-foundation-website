№ 01 THE STORY

Most people work hard to get more. We work hard because we were given more.

I was dealt incredible cards — a good family, good health, people who believed in me. I didn't earn that. So I treat it like a debt.

The plan is simple and it is not modest: build real businesses from nothing, document the whole climb, and give it all back — starting now, small and honest, and scaling as far as the businesses can carry it. Not someday. Every month, in public, with receipts.

The foundation is named after J.R. Parrish — the mentor who taught me what the cards were for.

№ 02 HOW IT WORKS
1

We build.

Real companies, built from zero, documented daily — wins, losses, and actual numbers. The businesses are the engine.

2

You give — directly.

The give button routes you to our official fundraiser page for SOS Children's Villages USA. Your money goes straight to them. It never touches our hands, ever.

3

Every dollar, counted.

Every dollar this mission generates is logged in the public ledger below — forever. The receipts are the whole point.

Honest fine print, up front: The Parrish Foundation is a giving mission, not yet an incorporated nonprofit. We don't collect donations ourselves — we fundraise for SOS Children's Villages USA, a registered 501(c)(3). Donations go directly to them, and your tax receipt comes from them.
