№ 01 OUR MISSION

One goal: raise $1,000,000 for children in need.

The Parrish Foundation isn't a fully established nonprofit yet. So instead of waiting until it is, we're generating that million through an organization that already does this work at the highest level — SOS Children's Villages USA.

Every dollar we raise goes straight to them. It never passes through our hands, and all of it is tracked in public, right here. One day the Foundation will stand on its own — but the giving doesn't have to wait. It starts now.

№ 02 CHARITABLE GOALS

The first goal: one million dollars.

$1,000,000 generated for children who need a loving home — the orphaned and abandoned kids who were dealt the worst cards. That's the number on the front page, and every dollar toward it is counted in public.

It's the first of many. As the mission grows, the goals grow with it — more raised, more good done, and one day the Parrish Foundation standing on its own. But it all starts here, with the first million.

№ 03 HOW IT WORKS
1

We build.

Real companies, built from zero, documented daily — wins, losses, and actual numbers. The businesses are the engine.

2

You give — directly.

The give button routes you to our official fundraiser page for SOS Children's Villages USA. Your money goes straight to them. It never touches our hands, ever.

3

Every dollar, counted.

Every dollar this mission generates is logged in the public ledger up top — forever. The receipts are the whole point.

Honest fine print, up front: The Parrish Foundation is a giving mission, not yet an incorporated nonprofit. We don't collect donations ourselves — we fundraise for SOS Children's Villages USA, a registered 501(c)(3). Donations go directly to them, and your tax receipt comes from them.

№ 04 WHO WE FUNDRAISE FOR

SOS Children's Villages USA

We picked one cause and we're sticking to it: kids who were dealt bad cards — orphaned and abandoned children who need what every kid deserves: a loving home. SOS Children's Villages is the world's largest organization doing exactly that.
